About Us

A Gym Built for Students & Beginners

Bakal Gym is a local fitness gym built for students, beginners, and fitness enthusiasts who want an affordable and supportive place to train. Whether your goal is to gain muscle, lose weight, improve endurance, or stay active, Bakal Gym provides the space, equipment, and guidance to help you start.

We believe fitness should be accessible to everyone — not just those with big budgets. That's why we offer student-friendly membership plans, beginner coaching, and a community that motivates you to keep going.

How It Works

Payment Process

Simple, secure, and straightforward — here's what happens after you choose a plan.

1

Choose a Plan

Select the membership plan that fits your budget and fitness goals.

2

Secure Payment

You are redirected to a secure payment gateway — GCash, Maya, card, or cash at the gym. We never collect your card details directly.

3

Get Confirmation

After successful payment, you receive a confirmation receipt or reference number via email or SMS.

4

Visit the Gym

Show your receipt or reference number at the gym. Our staff will verify your payment.

5

Get Your Member ID

Receive your membership card or digital confirmation. Your membership starts from verification date.

🔒
Your payment is secure.

Your payment is processed through a secure payment gateway. Bakal Gym does not store your card details. After payment, you will receive a confirmation receipt or reference number. Present it at the gym to claim your membership card or activate your membership.
